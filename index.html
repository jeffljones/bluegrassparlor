A little about us

We are a group of bluegrass bands and artists that have come together to help bluegrass fans everywhere lean about and appreciate bluegrass music. The Bluegrass Parlor has always been about sharing the good news of bluegrass music with everyone, old fans and newcomers alike. In years past that took the form of the Bluegrass Parlor music store or the Bluegrass Parlor Band. These days our focus has shifted from the store or the one band, but we are still doing our best to meet fans whever they are, be that social media, festivals, jams sessions, or via live streams.

We bring great bluegrass music to you.

Pick

Ever want to learn to play an instrument or sing harmony? We have an awesome group of teachers who can help you learn.

Grin

Want to find the best bluegrass music in all of Florida. This is the place to keep up with bands like Born Lonesome, Gypsy Wind, and Thump Barrel. Find out about all the latest gigs, and get access to exclusive live streams.

Represent

Want to support one or your favorite bluegrass bands or artists? This is the place to get all types of Band merchandise, buy music, and become a subscribing member to help support our artists continue to make and share great buegrass music..
